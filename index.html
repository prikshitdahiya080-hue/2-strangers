Ek hi school ki raahon mein chalte the hum,
Ek hi shehar ki hawaaon mein palte the hum.
Ghar bhi paas the, raaste bhi paas the,
Phir bhi na jaane kyun hum ek dusre se anjaan the.

Shayad kismat ko apni kahani likhne ka intezaar tha,
Do dilon ko ek karne ka khayal tha.

Phir ek din tum mili...

Na koi shor hua, na aasman badla,
Bas ek chhoti si baat hui, aur sab kuch badal gaya.
Dheere dheere alfaaz badhe, baatein gehri hui,
Aur anjaane mein dil ki duniya tumse hi bhari hui.
Jo ehsaas kabhi pehle mehsoos na hua tha,
Woh tumhare saath har roz hone laga tha.

Is berang si duniya mein ek rang nazar aaya,
Har bheed mein bas tumhara chehra nazar aaya.

Tum hastae toh lagta din khoobsurat ho gaya,
Tum udaas hoti toh dil bechain ho gaya.

Tumhari khushi meri dua ban gayi,
Tumhara dard meri khamoshi ban gayi.

Na jaane kaisa jaadu tha tumhari baaton mein,
Ki sukoon milne laga tumhari yaadon mein.

Har subah tumse shuru hone lagi,
Har raat tum par khatam hone lagi.
Phir ek din woh lamha bhi aaya,
Jab mohabbat ne humein apna naam bataya.

Hum ek aise bandhan mein jud gaye,
Jahan do dil ek hi dhadkan ban gaye.

Ab kahani sirf meri ya tumhari nahi rahi,
Do alag zindagiyan ek safar ban gayi.

Do ajnabi jo kabhi ek dusre ko jaante tak nahi the,
Aaj ek dusre ke bina khud ko pehchaante nahi.
Aur shayad mohabbat ka matlab bhi yahi hai...

Ki do anjaan log milte hain,
Dheere dheere ek dusre ki aadat bante hain,
Phir zaroorat bante hain,
Aur ek din...

❤️ Do anjaan ek dusre ki jaan ban jaate hain ❤️
